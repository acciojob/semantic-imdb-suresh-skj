Plot Summary

Inception is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for planting an idea into a target's mind, a process known as inception.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.

Favorite Quote

"Dreams feel real while we're in them. It's only when we wake up that we realize something was actually strange." - Dom Cobb
